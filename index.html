Why is Jennifer L. Armentrout one of my favorite authors?

I've read most of her books (sadly not all of them though), and I have loved all of them. From blood and ash being my favorite so far. She writes books from all different genres and she does it well. from fantasy to paranormal to contemporary, it doesn't matter they're all wonderful reads.
